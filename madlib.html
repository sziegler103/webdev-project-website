Mad Lib

Mad Libs are fill-in-the-blank word games. The player is presented with a series of blanks to be filled by a particular type of word (typically a noun, verb, adjective, or adverb). After the blanks are filled, the story is read aloud, sometimes to humorous effect.

With the implementation of Mad Libs here, you will fill in the following text boxes with the word-type asked for, and click the "Generate Story" button when you're done. The story will appear with your words filled in.
